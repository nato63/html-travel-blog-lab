A summary of a visit to Fire Island

An adventure to Fire Island was money well spent, I enjoyed every time spent there from the beautiful sun rise and sunset, delicious food, car free streets, tower viewing amongst other activities. Apart from all that the hospitality was just five star, good people they are free and friendly with visitors. If you're looking for a peaceful and quiet place with a lot of adventure, that's away from the busy city streets then you should definitely visit Fire Island.
